### **Product Name: TradieMate**

**Tagline:** "Fair dinkum job management for Aussie tradies"

### **Core Problem Solved:**

* 80% of tradies waste 10+ hours/week on admin (quotes, invoices, scheduling)  
* Lost revenue from forgotten follow-ups and unbilled work  
* Unprofessional paper quotes lose jobs to competitors  
* Can't track job profitability until it's too late  
* Existing tools (ServiceM8, Tradify) are $49-99/month and overwhelming

### **Target Customers (Niche First Approach):**

1. **Primary:** Solo tradies (electricians, plumbers, carpenters) doing $200k-500k/year  
2. **Secondary:** Small crews (1 tradie \+ 1-2 helpers)  
3. **Tertiary:** Handyman services and maintenance businesses

### **Why Australia First:**

* 411,000 tradie businesses in Australia  
* High smartphone adoption (95%+ of tradies have iPhone/Android)  
* Strong tradie community culture (word-of-mouth works)  
* Existing tools are expensive and complex  
* Less competition than US/UK markets  
* Average tradie willing to pay $29-49/month for time savings
